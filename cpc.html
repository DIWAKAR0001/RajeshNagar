चाय पर चर्चा: A Unique Connection

"Chai Par Charcha" holds different meanings for everyone. For me, it's a way to connect with my Field Force. Every Saturday and Sunday morning, I visit their homes to personally recognize and reward achievers. These visits allow us to discuss their visions and ideas for LIC's growth, fostering innovation and strengthening our team bond. Through "Chai Par Charcha," I celebrate our successes and align our goals, making it a cherished tradition that enhances our work culture.
